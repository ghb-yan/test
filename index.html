Memory Test - Improve Your Memory Skills

Welcome to the Memory Test Game!

The Memory Test is an engaging and educational online game designed to challenge and improve your memory. With 20 levels of increasing difficulty, this game will test your ability to remember numbers quickly and accurately.

How to Play

Each level presents a number for you to memorize within a 5-second time limit. Once the time is up, you will be prompted to enter the number you just saw. The numbers start from a single digit and increase in length as you progress through the levels.

Scoring System

Your score is calculated based on the number of levels you complete correctly. Each level has a different point value, starting from 5 points for the first level and increasing by 5 points for each subsequent level (e.g., Level 1 = 5 points, Level 2 = 10 points, Level 20 = 100 points). The maximum possible score is 1050 points.

Levels and Progress

The game consists of 20 levels. A progress bar at the top of the game screen shows your progress through the levels. Each completed level is marked with a green circle, while incorrect answers are marked with a red circle.

Gameplay Tips

  • Focus on the number presented and try to visualize it in your mind.
  • Practice regularly to improve your memory skills over time.
  • Stay calm and avoid rushing, as accuracy is more important than speed.
